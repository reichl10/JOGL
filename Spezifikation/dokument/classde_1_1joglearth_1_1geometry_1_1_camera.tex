\subsection{\-Ausführliche \-Beschreibung}
\-Administers geometrical calculations for the viewport perspective.

\-This class is not thread-\/safe by design, as clients will usually perform multiple dependent operations on the camera. \-In multi-\/threaded code, these blocks of operations should be synchronized on the camera object.
