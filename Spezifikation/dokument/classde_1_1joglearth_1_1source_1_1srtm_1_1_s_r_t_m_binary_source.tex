\subsection{Ausführliche Beschreibung}
Loads S\-R\-T\-M data from N\-A\-S\-A servers. The level of detail of a pixel in the S\-R\-T\-M tiles is 90 x 90 meters. The S\-R\-T\-M tiles include all information of a required point about the height. As 'standard elevation zero' the W\-G\-S84 spheroid is used. Only necessary if the Hight\-Profile is activated.
